If you want to understand a company,
don't listen to what it says.

Look at what it measures.

Many companies talk about:

- "best practices"
- "lean"
- "agile"

But what they actually measure is simple:

How much time you spend being "productive".

Which often means:

- no time for experiments
- no time for improvements
- no time for better engineering practices

And then everyone wonders why nothing really changes.
